English for IT

What is a computer?

▼

A computer is an electronic device that has the capability of storing and processing data. It uses the data given to it by the user to perform all kinds of calculations and tasks in the fastest way possible. Nowadays computers are used on a daily basis and are a fundamental piece in today's world, almost every part of society utilizes them in some way.

How do computers work?

▼

Computers work by receiving inputs, processing them and giving an output. Every click the user does, will impact and change the output. This is all achieved by electrical signals that are represented by binary code, that can be 0 or 1's.

What is the most important part?

▼

The most important part is the CPU or Central Processing Unit, also called the "brain" of the computer. This is what will determine how many inputs and outputs can be processed in a certain amount of time. The better the CPU, the faster the computer and without it the computer will not be able to operate. It is also responsible for the communication between all of the other components.

What are the components?

▼

The components are the CPU (Central Processing Unit), Motherboard, RAM (Random Access Memory), GPU (Graphics Processing Unit), Storage devices (HDD, SSD and more) and PSU (Power Supply).
